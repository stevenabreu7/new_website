Hi, I'm Steve!

PhD student at the University of Groningen

I am interested in interdisciplinary research around artificial intelligence: unconventional nature-inspired computing, understanding the human mind, and building wider artificial intelligence.

Currently working as a PhD student on non-digital computing theory with the goal of exploiting physical phenomena for computation - focused on neuromorphic hardware in particular. I joined the MINDS research group, funded through the European Commission's post-digital project.

I like to read, travel, take pictures, go to music concerts, and write about topics I care about.
